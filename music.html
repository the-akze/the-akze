I like producing music. I've been making music for 2-3 years.

I started in SoundTrap, an in-browser DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) where I would occasionally try making random beats. Some of my friends also started using SoundTrap. As I used it more and more, I started learning more techniques and essentials of producing music and getting the hang of it.

Later, I moved to GarageBand Mac, which I make my music on as of now. Although it might be a beginner music production software on the outside, you can make professional and good sounding music with it, which I have learned how to do.

Now, I make mainly electronic, lo-fi, and trap music.
